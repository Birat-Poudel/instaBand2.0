ABOUT US

InstaBand is a revolutionary app that can help connect you to thousands of talented musicians who can play for any of your upcoming occasions or events. With one click, you will see multiple bands available for you big day. We are an app designed to be the channel for allowing talented musicians to seek opportunities, connect with other... bands and people, and have the ability to borrow and share instruments wherever you are.
